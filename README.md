# NYC Taxi Trip Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
Interactive Excel dashboard built on 900,000 NYC taxi trip records (Jan–Mar 2026) to analyze revenue trends, peak demand periods, and zone-level pickup insights.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Power Query, Slicers, Charts)

## Dataset
- 900,000 taxi trip records
- Columns: Trip date, pickup zone, fare amount, tip amount, payment type, MTA tax, tolls, surcharge

## Key Insights
- Afternoon trips (12PM–6PM) drive 38% of total revenue
- Thursday and Friday are peak revenue days
- Card payments generate 25% average tip vs ~0% for cash
- Midtown Center and JFK Airport are the top pickup zones
- Revenue grew +4% from January to March

## Dashboard Features
- 6 KPI Cards: Total Revenue, Avg Fare, Avg Tip %, Total Trips, MTA Tax, Total Tolls
- 4 Charts: Top 10 Zones (bar), Revenue by Day of Week (column), Phase of Day (pie), Monthly Trend (line)
- 3 Interactive Slicers: Phase of Day, Day of Week, Payment Type
